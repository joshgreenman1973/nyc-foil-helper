This is an AI-generated draft to save you time — not legal advice. Read it, fix any placeholders in [brackets], and confirm the agency is right (an alternative may be listed above). FOIL requires an agency to acknowledge your request within five business days. If records are withheld, the agency must cite a specific exemption, and you have 30 days to appeal a denial to that agency's FOIL appeals officer.
